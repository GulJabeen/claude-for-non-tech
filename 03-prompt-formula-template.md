## The 3-part formula

Every great prompt has three pieces. Copy the structure below, fill it in, and paste into Claude.

```
Role: You are [who Claude should be]
Task: [exactly what to do]
Context: [everything Claude needs to know]
```

---

## 5 filled-in examples

**1. Writing a professional email**
```
Role: You are an expert business writer
Task: Write a follow-up email after a sales call
Context: I met with a potential client (Aisha, founder of a bakery) yesterday. 
We discussed a 3-month social media package at £800/month. She seemed interested 
but said she'd think about it. Keep the email warm, confident, and under 150 words.
```

**2. Writing an Instagram caption**
```
Role: You are a social media copywriter for lifestyle brands
Task: Write an Instagram caption for a product launch post
Context: I'm launching a new hand cream called "Soft Reset." It smells like 
green tea and aloe. My audience is women aged 25–40 who care about self-care. 
Tone: friendly, slightly poetic. End with a question to boost comments.
```

**3. Writing a client proposal**
```
Role: You are a freelance consultant who writes winning proposals
Task: Write a one-page proposal for a website redesign project
Context: The client is a local restaurant (Mango & Mint) that wants a modern 
site with online booking. Budget is around £2,000. I'm a freelance web designer 
with 5 years of experience. Make it confident and easy to read.
```

**4. Summarising a long document**
```
Role: You are an expert at turning dense text into clear summaries
Task: Summarise the following report in 5 bullet points
Context: Each bullet should be one sentence. Avoid technical jargon. 
The audience is non-technical managers. [paste your document below]
```

**5. Creating a 30-day content plan**
```
Role: You are a content strategist for small businesses
Task: Create a 30-day Instagram content plan
Context: My business is a home organisation service. I post 5 times a week. 
My audience is busy mums who want their homes to feel calmer. Mix educational 
posts, before/after photos, tips, and 2 promotional posts per month.
```

## Bad prompt vs. good prompt

| Situation | Bad prompt | Good prompt |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Writing help | "Write a caption" | "Role: Instagram copywriter. Task: Write a caption for a new product. Context: It's a gold ring, my audience is women 25–35, tone is luxurious and aspirational." |
| Summarising | "Summarise this" | "Role: Clear writer. Task: Summarise in 3 bullets. Context: Each bullet max 15 words, no jargon, for a non-technical audience." |
| Planning | "Make me a plan" | "Role: Business strategist. Task: 4-week launch plan. Context: I'm launching an online course about Canva. Budget: £0. I have 500 Instagram followers." |
