## What is MCP?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is the system that lets Claude connect to your real apps — so instead of just writing for you, Claude can actually read your emails, update your calendar, or edit your Notion pages directly.

---

## Top 10 most useful plugins

| Plugin | What it does | Best for |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Gmail** | Reads, drafts, and sends emails from your Gmail account | Drafting replies, summarising your inbox, writing cold outreach |
| **Google Drive** | Reads and creates files in your Drive | Summarising documents, drafting reports, finding files by asking |
| **Notion** | Creates, edits, and organises pages in Notion | Building wikis, writing notes, managing your knowledge base |
| **Google Calendar** | Reads your calendar and schedules events | Scheduling meetings, planning your week, spotting conflicts |
| **Slack** | Reads messages and posts to channels | Summarising threads, drafting announcements, replying to DMs |
| **GitHub** | Reads code, creates issues, and manages repos | Reviewing code, writing documentation, tracking bugs |
| **Zapier** | Connects Claude to 6,000+ other apps | Automating workflows between any tools you use |
| **Canva** | Creates designs directly from a prompt | Social posts, presentations, flyers — without opening Canva |
| **HubSpot** | Reads and updates your CRM contacts and deals | Writing follow-ups, updating deal stages, pulling contact info |
| **Google Sheets** | Reads and writes spreadsheet data | Analysing data, filling in columns, generating reports |
