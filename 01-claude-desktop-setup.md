## What is Claude Desktop?

**claude.ai** is the website — you open it in your browser like Google or YouTube.  
**Claude Desktop** is the app you install on your computer — it runs faster, stays open in your taskbar, and unlocks extra features like Projects and Skills that the website doesn't have.

---

## Step-by-step: Install Claude Desktop

1. Go to **https://claude.com/download**
2. Click **Download for Mac** or **Download for Windows** — whichever matches your computer
3. Open the downloaded file (it will be in your Downloads folder)
4. Drag the Claude icon into your Applications folder (Mac) or run the installer (Windows)
5. Open Claude Desktop — sign in with your email or Google account

---

## After install: Create your first Project

A Project is a dedicated space where Claude remembers your context every time you return. Think of it as a folder with a built-in memory.

1. In Claude Desktop, click **Projects** in the left sidebar
2. Click **New Project**
3. In the Project Instructions box, type a few sentences about who you are and what you need — for example:
   > I run a small online clothing brand. My customers are women aged 25–40. My tone is friendly and warm. Help me with writing, content, and customer messages.

Now every conversation inside that Project will already know this context — you never have to re-explain yourself.
