# Claude for Non-Tech — Episode 18: Live Artifacts
**Series:** Claude for Non-Tech by @techwithgul | **Keyword:** ARTIFACT

---

## What is Artifacts?

Artifacts is Claude's live canvas where it builds interactive tools next to your chat — instead of giving you text to copy, Claude creates a working spreadsheet, chart, calculator, or form that you can click and use immediately.

---

## How to open Artifacts

You don't need to turn anything on. Just ask Claude to build something — the Artifact appears automatically in a panel next to your conversation. When you want to tweak it, describe the change in the chat and Claude updates it live.

---

## 5 copy-paste Artifact prompts

---

### 1. Weekly Task Tracker

Copy and paste this into Claude:

```
Build me an interactive weekly task tracker. 
Columns: Task, Day, Priority (High/Medium/Low), Done (checkbox).
Pre-fill with 5 example tasks.
```

**What you get:** a clickable table where you can check tasks off as done, sorted by priority. Change the day or priority by clicking directly in the cell.

---

### 2. Revenue Chart

Copy and paste this into Claude (replace the numbers with yours):

```
Build me a bar chart showing monthly revenue.
Months: Jan–Jun. Values: [paste your numbers].
Use a clean, professional colour scheme.
```

**Example with real numbers:**
```
Build me a bar chart showing monthly revenue.
Months: Jan–Jun. Values: £4,200 / £3,800 / £5,100 / £6,400 / £5,900 / £7,200.
Use a clean, professional colour scheme. Add the total at the top.
```

**What you get:** a visual bar chart you can screenshot and share in a report or presentation.

---

### 3. Client Proposal Template

Copy and paste this into Claude:

```
Build me a professional client proposal template.
Sections: About Us, The Problem, Our Solution, Timeline, Pricing, Next Steps.
Make it editable.
```

**What you get:** a clean, formatted proposal document inside Claude where you can click each section and type your own content. Download or copy it when you're done.

---

### 4. Simple Calculator

Copy and paste this into Claude (choose your type):

```
Build me a [profit margin / invoice / budget] calculator.
Inputs: [list what numbers the user enters].
Show the result clearly at the top.
```

**Filled-in example — profit margin calculator:**
```
Build me a profit margin calculator.
Inputs: Revenue (£), Cost of Goods (£), Other Expenses (£).
Calculate: Gross profit, net profit, and profit margin percentage.
Show the result clearly at the top in large text.
```

**What you get:** a working calculator with input boxes. Type in your numbers and results update instantly.

---

### 5. Content Calendar

Copy and paste this into Claude:

```
Build me a monthly content calendar for Instagram.
Show 4 weeks. Each day has: Topic, Format (Reel/Post/Story), Status (Draft/Ready/Posted).
```

**What you get:** a full 4-week grid where you can fill in your content plan, change the format type, and track status for every post. Much faster than building it in a spreadsheet.
